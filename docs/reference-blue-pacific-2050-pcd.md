# Reference — Blue Pacific 2050, People-Centred Development (Thematic Area 2)

**Source:** https://blue-pacific-2050.pacificdata.org/people-centred-development
(Pacific Islands Forum / Pacific Data Hub). Retrieved 2026-07-02.
This is one of the 2050 Strategy's **seven thematic areas** (the others: Political
Leadership & Regionalism; Peace & Security; Resource & Economic Development;
Climate Change & Disasters; Ocean & Environment; Technology & Connectivity).

**Why it's here:** a real-world results framework whose shape matches RaiVanua's
Outcome Framework almost exactly — **Goal → Outcome → Indicator (with target)** —
useful as a template / import source for the Scorecard. Baselines and actuals are
served live from the dashboard (JS) and are not captured here; the structure,
targets and indicator codes are.

## B. Full Goal → Outcome → Indicator hierarchy (the backbone)

Indicator codes in brackets are the dashboard's series codes (mostly SDG series).

### Goal 1 — Improved health and well-being of all Pacific peoples
- **1.0 Improved health outcomes for all Pacific Peoples, particularly vulnerable communities and marginalised groups**
  - Maternal mortality ratio (SH_STA_MORT)
  - Under-five mortality rate (SH_DYN_MORT)
  - Tuberculosis incidence (SH_TBS_INCD)
  - Malaria incidence per 1,000 population at risk (SH_STA_MALR)
  - Mortality attributed to cardiovascular disease, cancer, diabetes or chronic respiratory disease (SH_DTH_NCD)
  - Mortality attributed to unsafe water, sanitation and lack of hygiene (SH_STA_WASH)
  - New HIV infections per 1,000 uninfected population (SH_HIV_INCD)
- **1.1 Strengthened institutional capacity and capability to address national and regional health priorities**
  - International Health Regulations (IHR) capacity (SH_IHR_CAPS)
  - Multi-sectoral NCD taskforce (BPI_MANAL1)
  - National strategy addressing NCDs and risk factors (BPI_MANASL2)
  - National guidelines for care of main NCDs (BPI_MANAGL2)
- **1.2 Strengthened coverage, quality, accessibility, affordability, and resilience of health and wellbeing services**
  - Births attended by skilled health personnel (SH_STA_BRTC)
  - Coverage of essential health services (SPC_3_8_1)
  - Population using an improved drinking water source (SH_H2O_IMPR)
  - Population using safely managed sanitation services (SH_SAN_SAFE)
  - Wastewater safely treated (SPC_6_3_1)
  - Health worker density (SH_MED_DEN)
  - Service coverage for mental health (BPI_CMH)
- **1.3 Improve healthy lifestyles across all communities, including increased participation in sports and physical activity**
  - Alcohol consumption per capita (15+) within a calendar year (SH_ALC_CONSPT)
  - Age-standardised prevalence of current tobacco use (15+) (SH_PRV_SMOK)
  - Healthy food policies in schools (BPI_MANAF5)
  - Compulsory physical education in school curriculum (BPI_MANAP1)

### Goal 2 — Improved educational achievement of Pacific learners through quality, accessible, affordable, and inclusive education at all levels
- **2.0 Improved educational achievement of all Pacific learners**
  - Children/young people achieving minimum proficiency in reading and mathematics (SE_TOT_PRFL)
  - Population achieving at least a fixed level of proficiency in literacy and numeracy (SPC_4_6_1)
- **2.1 Strengthened institutional capacity and capability to deliver Pacific education outcomes**
  - Trained teachers (SE_GC_TCAQ)
  - Global citizenship / education for sustainable development mainstreamed at all levels (SPC_4_7_1)
- **2.2 Strengthened Pacific education curriculum and assessment systems to meet employment and national development needs**
  - Completion rate, upper secondary (CR_3)
  - Adolescent birth rate (per 1,000 women aged 15-19) (SP_DYN_ADKL)
- **2.3 Improved access to quality, relevant, equitable, accessible, affordable, and inclusive formal and non-formal education for all Pacific learners, including learners with disabilities**
  - Schools with access to electricity (SE_ACS_ELECT)
  - Schools with access to computers for pedagogical purposes (SE_ACS_CMPTR)
  - Schools with access to basic drinking water (SE_ACS_H2O)
  - Schools with basic handwashing facilities (SE_ACC_HNDWSH)
  - Schools with access to the internet for pedagogical purposes (SE_ACS_INTNT)
  - Schools with access to single-sex basic sanitation (SE_ACS_SANIT)
  - Schools with adapted infrastructure/materials for students with disabilities (SE_ACS_DISAB)
  - Participation in organised learning one year before primary entry age (SE_PRE_PARTN)
  - Gross enrolment ratio for tertiary education (GER_5T8)

### Goal 3 — The Pacific region realises human rights, gender equality, equity, and social inclusion
- **3.0 The human rights of all Pacific peoples are respected, protected, and realised**
  - Population subjected to physical violence in previous 12 months (VC_VOV_PHYL)
  - Population subjected to robbery in previous 12 months (VC_VOV_ROBB)
  - Population subjected to sexual violence in previous 12 months (VC_VOV_SEXL)
  - Population subjected to psychological violence in previous 12 months (VC_VOV_PSYCHL)
- **3.1 Strengthened institutional capacity and capability to promote and protect human rights, gender equality, equity, and social inclusion**
  - Children under 5 whose births are registered with a civil authority (SG_REG_BRTH)
  - Legal frameworks for gender equality — Area 1: overarching frameworks & public life (SG_LGL_GENEQLFP)
  - Legal frameworks for gender equality — Area 2: violence against women (SG_LGL_GENEQVAW)
  - Legal frameworks for gender equality — Area 3: employment & economic benefits (SG_LGL_GENEQEMP)
  - Legal frameworks for gender equality — Area 4: marriage & family (SG_LGL_GENEQMAR)
  - Implementation plan for UN Treaty Body / UPR recommendations, fully or partially resourced (SPC_PROXY_3)
- **3.2 Pacific women and girls, in all their diversity, are safe and have equitable equal share of resources, opportunities, and decision making**
  - Women (15-49) with family planning satisfied by modern methods (SH_FPL_MTMM)
  - Ever-partnered women/girls subjected to intimate-partner physical/sexual violence, last 12 months (VC_VAW_MARR)
  - Women/girls 15+ subjected to sexual violence by non-partner, last 12 months (SPC_5_2_2)
  - Time spent on unpaid domestic and care work (SL_DOM_TSPD)
  - Women in managerial positions (IC_GEN_MGTL)
  - Women making own informed decisions on sexual relations, contraception, reproductive health (SH_FPL_INFM)
  - Reproductive health policy/guidelines/protocols fully or partially resourced (SPC_PROXY_1)
  - Legal framework guarantees women's equal rights to land ownership/control (SPC_5_a_2)
- **3.3 The six pre-conditions to disability inclusion are recognised and applied in the Pacific**
  - Population covered by at least one social protection benefit (SI_COV_BENFTS)

### Goal 4 — The Pacific's diverse cultures, traditions, spirituality, and languages are visible, valued, safeguarded, and inform all national and regional development actions
- **4.1 Strengthened institutional capacity and capability to promote and safeguard Pacific culture, traditions, languages, practices, and arts outcomes**
  - National budget allocated to culture — protection, preservation (heritage), and promotion (SPC_PROXY_2)

## C. How this maps to RaiVanua's Outcome Framework

| Blue Pacific 2050 | RaiVanua Outcome Framework |
|---|---|
| Thematic Area (e.g. People-Centred Development) | a Government pillar / focus grouping |
| **Goal** (G1–G4) | an **Outcome** (long-term goal), or a focus-area grouping |
| **Outcome** (1.0, 1.2 …) | an **Outcome** (finer grain) |
| **Indicator** (+ SDG code) | an **Outcome indicator** (`outcome_indicators`) |
| target embedded in the row text | the indicator's **target_value** per node/period |
| the "Summary" table | the roll-up scorecard view |

If we want this as live data, it's a clean seed: one migration inserting the
Goals/Outcomes into `outcomes` and the indicators into `outcome_indicators`
(same pattern as `migrations/040_seed_outcomes_from_framework.sql`).
